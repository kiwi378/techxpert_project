Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard (3-5 business days) and express (1-2 business days) shipping options. International shipping is also available.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with all packaging.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email that you can use to track your package.

Can I pay in installments for computers or accessories?

Yes, we offer several installment options, including credit card installments and "Buy Now, Pay Later" services such as Atome or PayLater. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

How can I be sure the products are genuine?

We are authorized resellers of well-known brands such as ASUS, MSI, Intel, and AMD. All products are 100% original and come with official manufacturer warranties.
