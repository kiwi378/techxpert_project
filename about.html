Our Story

Founded in 2024, TechXpert PCs has been at the forefront of providing high-quality PC components and accessories to enthusiasts and professionals alike. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make building and upgrading PCs accessible to everyone.

Today, we're proud to be one of the leading providers of PC components, offering a wide range of products from top manufacturers around the world.
